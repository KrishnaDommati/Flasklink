The Notes feature allows users to easily manage their personal notes directly from the dashboard. Users can add new notes, view previously added notes, and delete notes they no longer need. This functionality provides a streamlined way to organize and track important information.

This feature ensures that users have a smooth and intuitive experience while managing their notes, keeping their personal information accessible and organized.
